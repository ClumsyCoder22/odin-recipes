White Chicken Chilli

Very quick and easy, a never-fail recipe. My family and friends just love it. Serve with corn chips, diced onions, and shredded Cheddar cheese.

  • 3 cans of great Northern Beans, undrained
  • 2 cans pinto beans, undrained
  • 2 cans corn, undrained
  • 3 onions, diced
  • 2 cans of chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon hot pepper sauce
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 cans diced green chile peppers, undrained
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Combine great Northern beans, pinto beans, corn, onions, chicken broth, rotisserie chicken, green chile peppers, hot pepper sauce, oregano, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a slow cooker.

  2. Step 2

    3. Cook on Low until flavors combine, about 8 hours.
